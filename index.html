Maker of Things

The making of something whether physical, digital or something else entirely, is the most exciting thing anyone can do. This is the outlet for Lewis to discuss and share all the things he has and will be making. Along with the many mistakes he'll be making along the way, so you can learn from them and not make the same mistake when you make things.

About

Lewis is a maker of things, whether it’s developing a web based project, creating a brand, writing a story to broaden the world building or working on a new technology. Lewis has been working in the digital space in Manchester, United Kingdom for several years now, focused on supporting the growth of several businesses in Manchester. But with the Covid-19 pandemic doing something a little bit different and more self fulfilling became the focus.

For 2021 Lewis set an objective to make 56 things during the year, these include starting a new brand along with developing a corresponding website and writing a story for one of his worlds. With a video created for each thing that is made, detailing the experience of creating it, which are released on youtube.

The videos focus on showcasing an entertaining view and the joys of creating something every week, while teaching on the mistakes made. Lewis also regularly posts written updates on his blog again focusing on teaching from mistakes made while making things and post on other social media which you can find below.
